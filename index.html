The Famous Audi RS6 Crash

In 2019, a tragic incident involving an Audi RS6 occurred on the German Autobahn. A 68-year-old driver was traveling at an estimated speed of 300 km/h (186 mph) when he collided with a semi-truck. The impact caused the vehicle to become airborne and wrap around a light pole. The driver was identified, but his body was found mutilated beyond recognition. The German police did not release his name to the press.
